When you find a photo you want to use, you can simply right-mouse click the picture, click Save As, and save it into into your A New Me folder.
If you wish to see the full sized version of the image, simply click on it and the image will open in a new window.
Remember that you need to select photos to replace each of the following parts of your head: eyes, hair, ears, mouth, nose.

Keep in mind that some of the full-sized images below are really large - like enormously large (some are over 3000 pixels wide). This was done on purpose so that you can control the amount of detail shown in each facial feature. If you select a facial part and it does not resize well to fit your face, simply choose another person. There are over 140 face images below, so everyone can locate multiple images to use - you have no excuse for not replacing all 5 parts of your face.

One more thing here: you are not limited to selecting parts from your own gender. You can use pieces from anyone.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             

A quick note about the above images:

These pictures were selected because the individuals in them were more-or-less looking directly into the camera. If your favorite celebrity is not here, don't yell at me - it is likely that they don't take headshots very well (a large number of celebrities never allow photographs to be taken from directly in front as they feel it makes them look bad). Additionally, some of the images above are not of the greatest quality, don't have very good lighting, or the person's head it tilted to one side. I included images like this on purpose. You will need to make adjustments to the new head parts so that they look natural on your face. You have a very wide selection of not only features but skin tones and expressions to choose from, so take the time to look over all of the images and select ones that fit best with your face.
